Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that has become popular all over the world. It consists of layers of pasta sheets, meat sauce, cheese, and sometimes vegetables. The pasta sheets are boiled and then layered with the meat sauce, which is usually made with ground beef, tomatoes, onions, and garlic. The cheese used in lasagna is usually a combination of ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan. The dish is then baked in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Lasagna is a hearty and comforting dish that is perfect for feeding a crowd or for a cozy family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • salt to taste (we recommend 1 ½ teaspoons)
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
