Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that has become popular all over the world. It consists of layers of pasta sheets, meat sauce, cheese, and sometimes vegetables. The pasta sheets are boiled and then layered with the meat sauce, which is usually made with ground beef, tomatoes, onions, and garlic. The cheese used in lasagna is usually a combination of ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan. The dish is then baked in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Lasagna is a hearty and comforting dish that is perfect for feeding a crowd or for a cozy family dinner.
